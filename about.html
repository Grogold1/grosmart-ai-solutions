About GROGOLD LTD

GROGOLD LIMITED is a pioneering Nigerian startup using Artificial Intelligence to revolutionize food waste management in agriculture. Our core mission is to reduce post-harvest losses, improve productivity, and ensure sustainability in the food value chain.

We offer AI-powered solutions such as early warning systems for crop diseases, advanced analytics for waste tracking, and smart recommendations for perishable food management.

Our flagship product is Grosmart AI Solutions, which integrates real-time data and machine learning to assist farmers and suppliers across North Central Nigeria.
